Chicken Biryani

Description

Chicken biryani is a flavorful rice dish cooked with spices, herbs, and marinated chicken.

Ingredients

  • Basmati rice
  • Chicken
  • Onions
  • Spices
  • Yogurt

Steps

  1. Marinate the chicken.
  2. Cook rice partially.
  3. Layer rice and chicken.
  4. Cook on low heat.
